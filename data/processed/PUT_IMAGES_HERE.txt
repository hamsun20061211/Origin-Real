Training data for triposr_finetune:

1) Put PNG/JPG/WEBP files in:  data/processed/images/

2) Optional captions:  data/processed/captions/<same_filename_stem>.txt

   OR use data/processed/manifest.jsonl (one JSON object per line).

3) If you used scripts/image_collector to fill data/raw, copy images into data/processed/images/

Then run train.py with --processed-dir data/processed
